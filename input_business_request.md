# Synthetic Business Request

The Operations team wants a dashboard that shows monthly utility invoice status by property.

They need to see which invoices are received, pending extraction, ready for review, approved, or blocked due to missing data.

The current process is manual and requires analysts to check multiple inboxes, spreadsheets, and billing tools. The team wants to reduce delays before invoices are approved for downstream billing.

## Key needs

- Track invoice status by property and month
- Highlight invoices requiring human review
- Show missing data reasons
- Provide exportable results for Finance and Accounting
- Use certified data sources where available
- Preserve auditability from source record to dashboard output

## Success criteria

- Analysts can identify blocked invoices without checking multiple systems
- Finance can export approved invoice status by month
- Missing data reasons are visible and standardized
- Records requiring review are not pushed downstream automatically
